Our Band

a. FirstBourne is a five piece arena rock/power metal band from Boston, MA. Specializing in captivating hooks and masterful musicianship, guitarist Mike Kerr, vocalist Adrienne Cowan, and bassist Ven Thangaraj teamed up with Sony Brazil’s producer and guitarist Jimmy Olivera to create their debut full length, “Riot”. Drummer Chris Dovas joined shortly after, adding to the ranks of Berklee-trained and award-winning musicians

FB members draw influences from an era of Area Oriented Rock/Metal where musicianship, appearance and good songwriting took center stage. While rock and metal today has a wide set of genres and sub-genres, FB writes music to appeal to a broad range of listeners. As such, songs are written with simple, understandable structures, singable melodies, meaningful lyrics, catchy hooks, and motivational tempos all produced by Jimmy, who produces dozens of artists for Sony yearly.

Songs are not overdone, overproduced and do not force an onslaught of notes at breakneck tempo on the listener.
